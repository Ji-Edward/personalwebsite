Jamaal Charles Injury

10/11/2015

MRI's have confirmed a torn ACL in Jamaal Charles' right knee during Sunday's game against the Bears on a non-contact play. The Kansas City Chief's running back will not return for the remainder of the season. Both Reid and Charles are keeping positive about his return for the 2016-2017 season.

Replacing such a high caliber back is nearly impossible; however, there are some waiver wire options this week to ease the impact of the loss of Jamaal Charles. Kansas City is looking to add more depth by bringing in former Saints running back Pierre Thomas and former Browns running back Ben Tate for a workout. In the meantime, the Chiefs will be relying on Charcandrick West who rushed seven times for 31 yards on Sunday. However, Reid likes both Charcandrick West and Kniles Davis due to their different run styles. They will likely share duties, at least for now, to fill in for Charles.
